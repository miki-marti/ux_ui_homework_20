iOS Mobile App UX Case Study:

Plate Theory




Overview:

We set out to build an app for people who want to order food from non-mainstream providers. The app had to add convenience to the userâ€™s lifestyle and be able to accommodate their dietary needs.


Challenge:

People with busy lifestyles that want alternatives to eating out need a structured way to search for home-based chefs and bakers.


Roles, Scope and Constraints:

Our team of three UX designers limited design scope to the perspective of the consumer due to time constraints.


Approach:

User-centered design process, with insights from user interviews serving as foundation. We used Figma and Miro as our primary tools.


Result:

Feedback from prospective users indicates design meets an unmet user need.
